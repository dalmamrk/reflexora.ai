The Reflex Layer

Our unique architecture features a dedicated Reflex Layer for low-latency, deterministic operation (microseconds to milliseconds). We develop next-generation electronic systems that deliver ultra-fast, automatic “reflex” responses directly at the sensor and edge level.

The Policy Layer

The Reflex layer seamlessly interfaces with a higher-level Policy Layer. This hierarchical design ensures rapid, reliable edge actions while maintaining intelligent governance, adaptability, and safety through sophisticated policy orchestration.
